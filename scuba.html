Scuba Diving

Introduction

During the trip, students will have the opportunity to earn their scuba diving certification. This certification shows that they have learned important safety skills and techniques to dive confidently and responsibly. It’s a valuable qualification that allows them to dive at many locations around the world and opens up new underwater adventures for the future. Getting certified adds an exciting and rewarding part to the scuba diving experience!

Schedule

Each day, students will complete three dives to explore different underwater spots. The day starts with two dives in the morning, followed by a lunch break to rest and recharge. In the afternoon, there will be one more dive before dinner. After dinner, the group will have a debrief session to talk about the day’s dives, share experiences, and learn from each other. This schedule balances adventure with plenty of time to relax and reflect.
