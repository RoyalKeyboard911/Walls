Sri Lanka

Introduction

Get ready for an exciting trip to Sri Lanka! Students will explore the beautiful mountains, enjoy nature, and experience the local culture. The group will go on hikes through lush green hills, visit peaceful temples, and see amazing wildlife. There will also be time to relax on the beach and learn about Sri Lankan food and traditions. This trip is a great mix of fun, learning, and adventure in one of the most beautiful countries in the world.

Schedule

During the trip to Sri Lanka, students will follow a fun and balanced schedule. Each day will start with breakfast at the hotel, followed by a main activity like hiking in the mountains, visiting a local temple, or exploring a nature reserve. In the afternoons, there will be time to relax at the beach or take part in cultural workshops, such as cooking or traditional dance. Evenings will include group dinners and time to reflect on the day. There will also be a day trip to a tea plantation and a guided tour through a historical city. Every day offers a new adventure, while keeping students safe, engaged, and inspired.
